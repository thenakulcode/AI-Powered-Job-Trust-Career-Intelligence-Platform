            About Sentinel

            How the detection pipeline works

            Sentinel analyzes job posting text with a TF‑IDF vectorizer feeding an XGBoost classifier trained on labeled real and fraudulent listings. Each scan runs entirely against your own request — nothing is persisted server-side. Risk factors, threat indicators, and the recommendation shown after a scan are all derived directly from the model's own output, not simulated.
